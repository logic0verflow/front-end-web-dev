Hedgehog

A group of hedgehogs is called an "array."

  • Family Class: Erinaceinae
  • Average Lifespan: 4 years
  • Average Length: 10 inches
  • Quills: Non poisonous

Hedgehogs are primarily nocturnal animals that are immune to many forms of snake venom. A hedgehog can have over 5000 quills that are not poisonous and don't have barbs. Their quills are composed of tiny air chambers that make them strong but also light weight.
